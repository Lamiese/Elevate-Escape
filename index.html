WHAT THIS ACTUALLY IS

A travel business that fits around your life, not the other way round.

Every time someone books a flight, a hotel, a transfer, or a holiday package, commission is already built into the price. When you become an independent travel partner, you earn that commission instead of a stranger's booking site.

You don't need a background in travel, a big following, or hours of spare time. We train you from scratch, you work it around school runs, your current job, or your own schedule, and you're supported by a team that's already done it.

This isn't a side hustle that fizzles out after a month. It's a real business, with real structure, built by women who wanted more control over their time and their income.

QUESTIONS BEFORE YOU JOIN

The questions we get asked most.

Do I need experience in travel or sales?+
Not at all. Most of the team had never worked in travel before joining. We train you from the very basics, how to book, how to talk to clients, how to find your first few customers, so you're not figuring it out alone.
Is this a pyramid scheme?+
No. You earn commission on real travel bookings made through licensed partners, that's the core business. There's also an optional side where you can introduce others to the same opportunity and earn from that too, but it's entirely optional and separate from the booking income.
I already have a full time job and kids. Will this actually work for me?+
This is exactly who the business is built for. We have teachers, nurses, full time employed mums, and students on the team. There are no shift patterns and no quotas, you decide how many hours you put in and when.
Do I need a big social media following?+
No. Plenty of the team have built their entire business through friends, family, and word of mouth, with little to no social presence at all. A following can help, but it's not a requirement.
How and when do I actually get paid?+
Commission is paid out after the travel has taken place, typically within 30 to 90 days, once our host agency receives it from the supplier. We'll walk you through the exact split and timing on your call.
What if I join and it's not for me?+
You're not locked into a contract. If it's not the right fit, you can step away at any point. We'd rather you try it properly than feel trapped.
